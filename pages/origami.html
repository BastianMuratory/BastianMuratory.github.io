My Origami Folds

I remember being very young when my mother taught me how to make origami, beginning with the humble crane and progressing slowly toward more complex models.

I feel like I've always loved the patience and creativity around this art, but as i get older, I enjoy more and more the science required to transform a single square sheet of paper into absolutely anything.

On this page you'll find almost all the origami I've folded in my life, along with the original artist who designed each model.
