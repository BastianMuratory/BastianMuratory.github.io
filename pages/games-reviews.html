My Video Game Reviews

As far as I remember, I've always been a gamer. I remember playing with my dad after looking at him play Balance on the familly computer. But also dying again and again on new super mario bros the first time I got my hand on a DS. The countless nights playing with friend after school, always trying to stay 5 more minutes hopping mom wouldn't notice. Until today where I tend to enyoy more puzzly or story driven games.

Being interested in user interfaces and experiences, I want to share what I think about the different games I play.

Keep in mind all the games here are the one I finished. Which means I still enjoyed enough 1 star review games to finish them. 5 stars games on the other hand are the ones that really are special to me in some way, wether I keep coming back to them or they just played too big of a role in my gamer life.
